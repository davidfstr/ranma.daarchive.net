Child of the Goddess

Ever so slowly the sun peaked over the horizon as morning dawned upon the Masaki Shrine. The lake glimmered in the morning light while songbirds sang their lovely songs. It was a picture of peace and tranquility.

Two log guardians sat at the entrance to the Masaki compound. Both vigilant while they admired the beauty of nature around them.

"Ah, what a beautiful morning." said Azaka.

"That it is. Such a peaceful one too." said Kamidake.

"Indeed" replied Azaka.

A few birds flew overhead on their way to perch upon the Masaki household. Inside resided several of the most powerful beings on the planet. In one room, a young man slowly awoke as the rays of sunshine shone in his face. His name was Tenchi Masaki. He was the grandson of the nearby shrine's priest. And he also happened to be the champion of a certain kind hearted goddess.

Sitting up, Tenchi rubbed the sleep out of his eyes as he slowly got out of bed. Grabbing a few things, he groggily he made his way to the bathroom to clean up for the day ahead.

Meanwhile, in another room, a certain martial artist began to wake. Slowly her eyes opened to gaze upon the ceiling. She smiled as she recalled spending time with her mother the previous evening. A slight movement to her side caused her to freeze momentarily. Looking down at the bundle that was snuggled up to her, she smiled and relaxed a little. Sasami was cuddled up to Ranma's side while sleeping peacefully. Bringing her hand up, Ranma stroked Sasami's hair a few times.

The slight movement of Ranma awoke a certain friendly little cabbit. Yawning a little, Ryo-Ohki blinked away her sleepiness and looked around. She saw that her bed was stroking the hair of her favorite person. Wanting to be pet too, Ryo-Ohki decided to greet the new comer.

{"Good Morning"} said Ryo-Ohki. Although to most it would have come out as a simple Miyahh, to Ranma it sounded like a female was saying good morning.

"Huh?" said Ranma wondering who spoke.

Looking around she couldn't see anyone other than Sasami who was still asleep. Feeling something moving on her belly, she looked down to see Ryo-Ohki, looking at her. Wondering about it a moment, Ranma's question was answered by Ryo-Ohki speaking again.

{"Hello."} said Ryo-Ohki.

Blinking a moment, Ranma said, "Um... Hello."

Curious, Ryo-Ohki could swear her perch had understood her. But from her past experience, the only ones that could understand her were Ryoko and Washu. But that was from a mental link with them. And even that was limited. Deciding to test something, Ryo-Ohki spoke again.

{"Do you understand me?"} asked the little cabbit hopefully.

Blinking some more, Ranma said, "Um... Yeah. How come you can talk?"

Her test confirmed, Ryo-Ohki cheered happily. Someone that she could talk to finally. She could understand people easily but communicating back was always something she couldn't do. Now she had found someone that could understand her. She was very happy.

Ranma watched as the little cabbit became really happy. Was it because she could understand the little creature? From the sounds of the little cabbit, that began to sound like what the case was.

"Are you ok?" asked Ranma.

Stopping in her celebration, Ryo-Ohki looked back at Ranma. Nodding, Ryo-Ohki responded, {"Yes I am. I've never really had anyone understand me before besides Ryoko-neechan and Washu-kasan."}

"Washu is your mom and Ryoko is your sister?" asked Ranma.

Shaking her head, Ryo-Ohki said, {"They are not related to me. I just call them that because of how I view them."}

"Ah. I've met Washu. She seemed nice. Ryoko I've only met briefly." said Ranma.

Sitting on her haunches, Ryo-Ohki asked, {"Would you like to be my friend?"}

Smiling, Ranma nodded as best she could. Seeing the cabbit's reaction, Ranma smiled some more. For some reason she liked the little creature. It sounded like a cat but didn't quite look like one. She wondered if it was because of the Neko-Ken that she could understand the little creature. The panther did say that she'd be able to communicate with cats as well as have a deep love for them. Perhaps that was why she found herself falling for the antics of the little cabbit.

"What's your name?" asked Ranma.

Happy that she had made a new friend, Ryo-Ohki said, {My name is Ryo-Ohki. How about you?"}

"My name is Ranma." replied Ranma.

Curious again, Ryo-Ohki asked, {"I thought Sasami called you Rhea."}

Chuckling lightly, Ranma said, "Well that was my name in my previous life. You see, I was born on Jurai to a woman named Tsunami."

Surprise shone on her features, Ryo-Ohki said, {"So you're Tsunami-sama's daughter. No wonder Sasami was so happy."}

Smiling, Ranma said, "Well I'm more than her daughter. I'm also her son."

Confused, Ryo-Ohki said, {"Son? But you are female."}

Chuckling a little, Ranma said, "Well I was born male. I acquired a curse that turns me female when I get splashed with cold water. Hot water will return me to my male form."

Still confused, Ryo-Ohki said, {"But how?"}

"I'll explain later. For now I think I should get up. It's going to be a busy morning and I have a few things to do." said Ranma.

About that time, Sasami began to wake. Opening her eyes, she saw Ranma and Ryo-Ohki... Talking?

"Oneeniichan? Who are you talking to?" said Sasami as she rubbed her eyes.

Looking over at Ryo-Ohki a moment, the little cabbit Miyahh'd.

{"You can tell her"} said the cabbit. However only Ranma understood her.

Both Ranma and Sasami sat up. Smiling at Sasami, Ranma said, "Good Morning imouto-chan. I was just talking with Ryo-Ohki here."

"Talking? But how?" asked Sasami.

"I'm not too certain about that myself. I think it's from a technique I learned called the Neko-Ken. It allowed me to understand and communicate with cats and judging from Ryo-Ohki here, it would seem cat like animals as well." said Ranma.

"That's neat! I wanna learn the Neko-Ken so I can talk with Ryo-Ohki too!" said a now more awake Sasami. However her excited state was lost when she saw the look on Ranma's face.

"I'm sorry Sasami. I can't do that. I would not wish that horror upon anyone." said Ranma.

"Why? What's so bad about it oneeniichan?" asked Sasami.

Sighing, Ranma decided to tell her. Best to let her know before she tried to find out about it some other way.

Ryo-Ohki also perked up. She too wanted to know how someone could learn how to speak with her.

"You see Sasami, when I was really young, the man who raised me got ahold of a training manual for a martial arts technique called the Neko-ken. However he didn't read the whole manual. The training involved wrapping me up in various fish products and tossing me into a pit of starving cats. He kept doing it until I finally snapped and learnt the technique. It gave me an intense fear of anything feline and it was cause of him and that technique that my Juraian heritage was sealed away. It was only recently that I merged with the Neko-ken and gained the ability you wanted." said Ranma

By now Sasami was in tears. To be put through something that horrible while so young. She wanted nothing to do with it now. Her oneeniichan was right in not wanting to subject her to it. Wrapping her arms around the older girl, Sasami hugged her for all she was worth.

"That's horrible! I don't wanna learn it now." said a crying Sasami.

Ryo-Ohki miyahh'd her opinion too, {"If I ever get my claws on that man!"}

Smiling down at the little bundle in her arms, Ranma said, "It's alright. I didn't really want you to worry about me. That's why I didn't mention it earlier. Don't mention it to anyone either. Only those that need to know have any knowledge of it."

Nodding, Sasami wiped away her tears. If her oneeniichan said it was ok, then that was ok with her. Standing up, Sasami began to get ready for the day ahead. It was still fairly early and she had to make breakfast.

Ranma picked up Ryo-Ohki and stood up as well. Looking around a moment, she realized she left her pack up at the shrine. Stepping over to the door, Ranma opened it before turning around to say, "I'll be back Sasami. I need to get my pack from the shrine."

"Ok Ranma-neechan." said Sasami while fixing her hair.

Stepping out into the hallway, Ranma headed downstairs. Arriving at the door, she was met with the site of one Tenchi Masaki putting some shoes on.

"Hello" she said.

Tenchi, not expecting anyone to be up yet was quite startled. Turning around, he was met with the site of a female Ranma looking at him while holding Ryo-Ohki.

"Um... Hello. You're Ranma? Right?" said Tenchi.

Smiling, Ranma nodded, "Yeah. And I'd assume yer Tenchi."

Nodding, Tenchi replied, "Yeah."

"Sorry about last night. Yer grandpa put me up to it." said Ranma.

"It's alright. I'm just glad you could take care of yourself. Ryoko and Ayeka don't get along too well unless it involves defending me." said Tenchi.

"No Problem. They weren't that tough. So where are you off to so early in the morning?" asked Ranma.

"I'm going up to the shrine so I can practice with Grandpa." replied Tenchi.

"Mind if I tag along?" asked Ranma.

"I don't mind." replied Tenchi.

Stepping outside, the two began their journey toward the shrine.

Turning to Tenchi, Ranma asked, "So, what do you practice?"

"Grandpa's been teaching me how to use a sword for a while now. He's been teaching me Juraian sword forms lately." said Tenchi.

"A sword eh? You do know that weapons can be a crutch in battle if it's taken away from you. Do you know any unarmed fighting techniques?" said Ranma

Shaking his head, Tenchi replied, "Umm... Not really. I only know how to use a sword. But I can't really be disarmed either."

"Oh? How so?" asked Ranma.

Scratching the back of his head, Tenchi replied, "Well I can use the light hawk sword. It can't be taken away from me."

"Ah, so your the one mom was talking about. The one she gifted with the light hawk wings." said Ranma.

"Mom?" he asked himself before realization dawned on his face. The lengthy conversation from the previous night coming back to him.

"Oh yeah. You're Tsunami's daughter. Is it true that you are really a guy?" asked Tenchi.

Smiling a little, Ranma nodded. "Yeah, I was born on earth as a guy. Picked up the curse in China that turns me female."

"Yeah, Grandpa told us all about that last night. The two people that came with you filled in the missing details as well." said Tenchi.

"They didn't mention anything called the Neko-Ken did they?" asked Ranma. Her face very serious. Ryo-Ohki almost growled at the name of that technique.

"Umm... No. Not that I recall. Why?" said Tenchi.

"No reason. Just forget it." said Ranma.

Tenchi simply nodded.

By now the two were about halfway up the steps to the shrine. As they continued up the stairs, the two began to loosen up around one another. Each talking about various things they had experienced. From numerous fiancee's to battling intergalactic criminals, the two became fast friends. Ryo-Ohki just snuggled up to Ranma and listened to her stories.

*******************

Up at the shrine, three people sat sipping tea. All had risen early due to Katsuhito training Tenchi today. The two elders that accompanied Ranma wanted to see how good Tenchi was.

"So how long have you been teaching Tenchi?" asked Happi.

"For quite a few years now. Why?" replied Katsuhito.

"No reason. I was just curious. Have you taught him any advanced Juraian sword techniques?" said Happi.

"I've only recently begun teaching them to him. So far I've been teaching him basic sword forms and such. However he's shown that he is ready for the more advanced techniques." said Katsuhito.

Nodding, Happi said, "I'd like to see him for myself. After all, I was the head combat instructor for the palace security. Appointed by the emperor himself no less."

Blinking at the new information, Katsuhito said, "Really? You never mentioned it before. Just that you were Juraian. Perhaps we can compare notes on Tenchi's training later. But how did you end up like you are now?"

"I don't really want to talk about it." said Happi.

Smiling, Cologne said, "Come now Happi. Why don't you tell him how you stole a couple pairs of panties from that woman, Misaki."

Happosai paled as Cologne revealed what he had done all those centuries ago. Slowly he turned his head toward Katsuhito.

Noticing Happi's facial expression, Katsuhito began to chuckle a bit with Cologne joining in as well. Both soon broke down into laughter at the site. Happi however failed to see the joke.

After a moment, Katsuhito and Cologne calmed down a bit. "Do not worry. I do not care about what you've done in the past. All I ask is that you do not do it to any of the girls here. They would be quite upset and might do worse than mother did. Especially Sasami or Ayeka. They are both Misaki's daughters." said Katsuhito.

Nodding, Happi decided to keep quiet about that little incident from now on. Too many people were around that were connected to Misaki. Especially the two pretty ladies Katsuhito just mentioned.

It was about that time that two voices from outside alerted Katsuhito, Happi, and Cologne that there were visitors. Rising, Katsuhito opened the door to see Tenchi and Ranma walking toward the shrine talking adamantly about something. In Ranma's arms rested Ryo-Ohki.

Katsuhito smiled. It was too bad Ranma was born male. Most likely Ranma was only interested in girls. The two would have made a good couple. Especially with their long lifespans. They could have been together forever. Well, it was not to be. He knew that Ayeka and Ryoko were after Tenchi. He had also noticed that Ryoko was calming down and it appeared she was developing feelings for Ayeka as well. Only time would tell how things would turn out.

Tenchi and Ranma finished their conversation as soon as they noticed Katsuhito, Cologne, and Happosai watching them. Happi looked a little pale while Cologne was chuckling about something.

Reaching to the side, Katsuhito grabbed two bokkens. Tossing one to Tenchi, the old priest stepped down into the courtyard. Moving to one side, he brought up his bokken.

On the other side of the courtyard stood Tenchi, his own bokken at the ready. Ranma had moved over to the shrine to sit with the elders. All three visitors were curious as to how good Tenchi was. Happosai and Ranma paid the most attention. Cologne glanced briefly at the cabbit before returning her attention to the fight.

At some unknown signal, the two combatants rushed toward one another to meet with a loud crack as the bokkens connected. The two combatants fought back and forth. Each using basic sword skills. A slash here. A block there. This lasted for only a few minutes. It served as a sort of warm-up to the real training.

To the sidelines, Happosai analyzed the fight. *Hmm... The boy has potential. He knows how to use the sword but he is slightly hesitating. He seems to have a confidence problem. It's as if he's not confident in his own abilities. I will have to speak with the boy later. Yosho on the other hand is as good as they said he was. Powerful, graceful, and confident. A true warrior.*

Next to him, Ranma conducted his own analysis. *Wow. The old man could run circles around Kuno. He doesn't waste a lot of movement. All his strikes are perfect. Tenchi seems to be a little pressed to keep up. He's hesitating. Seems he's not confident enough in his skills. Maybe I can help him work on it later.*

While the two visitors watched the fight, Katsuhito and Tenchi had separated to rest a little and prepare for the next part. Katsuhito raised his bokken and spoke to Tenchi.

"Tenchi. I want to you execute the technique I taught you." said Katsuhito.

Ranma perked up at the mention of a technique. Perhaps it was one he didn't know?

Happi just shook his head when he noticed Ranma perk up.

Cologne simply smirked.

"Are you sure Grandpa? You might get hurt." said Tenchi.

"I'm sure. Now just do it." said Katsuhito.

"Alright" said Tenchi.

Concentrating, Tenchi gathered the energy needed for this Juraian sword technique. Once the energy was gathered, he willed it into the wooden blade.

"Tsume No Taka Kara Tengai!" shouted Tenchi as he brought his bokken forward.
(((Tsume No Taka Kara Tengai = Talons of the Hawk from Beyond the Heavens. I think. Someone verify me.)))

Blue energy lanced out forming three energy blades parallel with one another. They sped toward the awaiting priest kicking up dust and dirt as they went along.

Katsuhito prepared himself to stop the technique. He knew the counter to it and didn't want any of the trees to get damaged. He smiled inwardly as he remembered when he first showed Tenchi the technique. Tenchi didn't think he'd be able to do it. Day after day of practicing had shown that Tenchi did have what it took to learn the advanced Juraian sword techniques. He had advanced far enough to begin the advanced training.

Off to the side, Happosai smiled and nodded to himself. He knew that technique as well. It was one of the lesser Juraian sword techniques which channeled the power of Jurai.

Ranma however had an odd look in his eye. He'd seen a new technique and couldn't wait to try and learn it. It looked simple enough. Briefly he wondered if a sword was needed to execute it. From the appearance of the attack, it appeared he might be able to adapt it to work with the Neko-Ken.

Back at the battle, the energy blades had reached Katsuhito. Quickly he brought his bokken down in a horizontal slash. Under normal conditions, the bokken would have been sliced apart. However the piece of wood he held was imbued with the power of Jurai. As such he was able to slash the attack and interrupt its energy matrix causing it to dispel harmlessly into the air.

Tenchi breathed a sigh of relief as the attack was stopped. He didn't like using techniques like these. They were deadly and he preferred to be more peaceful. Although recent events had forced him to begin learning them. He also didn't care much for the fact he'd also been forced to kill. However it was a decision he could live with since the lives of his loved ones were in danger.

Katsuhito relaxed his stance and bowed to Tenchi before moving toward the shrine where the visitors were watching. Tenchi followed suit and came up next to his grandpa.

"So, what do you think?" asked Katsuhito.

Taking a puff of his pipe, Happosai said, "He show's promise. The technique was well executed. However he needs to stop hesitating. Such hesitation could mean the difference between life or death one day. He also needs to gain more confidence in himself."

Nodding, Katsuhito turned to Ranma, "And what did you think Ranma?"

"Well, he's way better than this one idiot back in Nerima who uses nothing but a sword. But I agree with the old man on this one. Tenchi needs to stop hesitating when he strikes. I also think he needs to learn some unarmed combat as well." said Ranma.

"I agree." said Katsuhito before turning to Tenchi. "Do you understand what they are saying Tenchi? You must not hesitate when you face an opponent. Your opponent could use your hesitation against you."

Nodding, Tenchi said, "Yes Grandpa. I just... Well... I didn't want to hurt you."

Placing his hand on Tenchi's shoulder, Katsuhito said, "You mustn't worry about such things Tenchi. You must learn to rely on your instincts. You have a good heart but you hold yourself back because you do not wish to do harm to others. That is a respectable quality and one to be proud of. However when in battle, that quality can become a liability."

"I know Grandpa. It's just that I don't like fighting like this. What if something happened and you got hurt severely. I don't know what I'd do." said Tenchi.

"Yo, don't worry about it too much Tenchi. Your gramps is right. When in battle you can't hesitate but that still doesn't mean you have to stop caring about people. I do it every time I'm in a fight with someone I care about. I won't hesitate to battle them but I won't cause them lasting harm either. Besides, your grandfather can take it." said Ranma.

Happosai nodded, "He's right. Some people will not back down just because you ask them to. Sometimes you must fight to protect those you care about. I've done it and I'm sure Katsuhito here has done it as well. Even Ranma here has had to do it."

Nodding, Tenchi said, "Ok. I'll try to work on it."

Turning to Ranma, Katsuhito asked, "Would you like to spar?"

Thinking about it a moment, Ranma shook his head. "I'd like to, but not right now. I have something more pressing to do and I'm afraid I'll need all my energy for it."

Nodding, Katsuhito said, "Well why don't we all go back to the house. Sasami should have breakfast finished soon."

Receiving nods all around, everyone began to head for the house. However Ranma stayed back for a moment.

"Hey Happosai. Hold up a moment. I need to talk with you privately." said Ranma.

Happi turned and nodded. Everyone else left since Ranma said it was private. Cologne however wondered what Ranma wanted to talk to Happi about. And what would Ranma need all his energy for? He was a literal powerhouse by her standards.

Ryo-Ohki sat on Ranma's shoulder and miyahh'd quietly. Ranma turned and smiled. "You can stay."

As soon as everyone was out of sight, Ranma sat on the porch of the shrine. Motioning him over, Ranma waited until Happi was situated.

"So what did you want to talk to me about my boy?" asked Happi.

Thinking a moment, Ranma said, "Last night I was visiting with my mother."

"You visited Tsunami-sama?" asked a slightly wide eyed Happosai.

Nodding, Ranma continued, "Yes. Sasami thought I'd like to see my mother. She took me there last night while I slept."

"So what happened?" asked Happi.

"We caught up with each other from the thousands of years we've been apart. She also taught me a few new things like transporting between trees and other ways of using my power." said Ranma.

Nodding, Happi asked, "Interesting. So what did you need to talk to me about?"

"Well, I asked her about what that Misaki lady did to you. She said she had wondered what had happened. She felt your connection to her diminish abut 300 years ago." replied Ranma.

"Go on." said Happi.

Chuckling a little, Ranma said, "Yeah, she was amused with the part of you stealing Misaki's panties. But she also said that Misaki went too far in punishing you. Only she has the right to remove the power of Jurai from someone."

A little pale that his goddess knows of his little acquisition, Happi nervously asked, "So what does this mean for me?"

Smiling, Ranma said, "Well it means that I can now undo what Misaki did to you. Mom taught me how to do what Misaki did and how to undo it. "

Happi looked up at Ranma, hope evident in his eyes. Finally his years of suffering were over. The power of Jurai would be his once again.

"But she did say there was one stipulation on it." said Ranma.

"What did she say?" asked Happi. He'd do anything to regain what Misaki took away.

Smirking a little evilly, Ranma said, "She said that you were to stop stealing women's undergarments as well as stop groping every girl you come across."

Happi knew the price would be high. But for it to be his favorite past time and hobby. A warrior's life was fraught with peril. Sighing, Happi simply nodded.

"Very well. From this day forth, I Happosai, of the house Agemi, shall no longer steal another piece of women's lingerie nor shall I grope a woman unless it is consensual. This I swear to my goddess and her child." stated Happi.

A little put off by the long winded speech, Ranma said, "Yeah, well a simple ok would have been enough. Anyways, let's get started. This is going to drain me a bit since I'm still working on building up my stores of Juraian power. Now, face me and close your eyes."

Nodding, Happosai faced Ranma and closed his eyes. Briefly before his eyes shut, he saw some kind of furry creature on Ranma's shoulder. Shrugging to himself, he decided he'd ask after this was done with.

Seeing that Happi was ready, Ranma reached forward and laid her hand upon Happi's forehead. Closing her eyes, Ranma concentrated and began to search for the nodes in Happi's body that needed to be opened to undo what Misaki did to him all those years ago.

In her mind's eye, Ranma saw the nodes that made up the energy network of Happosai. Many were bright and full of energy. Most likely energy he siphoned off girls he groped mused Ranma. Some of them were fading, probably due to not having a recharge in so long. Soon Ranma came to several nodes, all dull blue with some sort of blue energy surrounding them. Tsunami said they would look like this. The blue energy being Misaki's power keeping Happi from utilizing his birthright.

Ranma concentrated on the glowing power that surrounded the nodes. Using her own power, she cut Misaki's power away. It was easy since she was the child of Tsunami. Any one other than herself, her mother, or Misaki would not have been able to do this.

Once Misaki's power was removed, the nodes began to glow again. Blue energy pulsed out of them and filled Happosai's being. It was quite beautiful thought Ranma before she pulled her senses back in to her own body. Opening her eyes, she watched as Happosai began to glow. Shortly after his body began to grow taller while his age reduced. Soon knelt a man that looked about 35 years old. However he was much older in reality.

Happi felt his body changing after the surge of Juraian power. How much he missed having the power of Jurai within him. The human energy within his body left and was replaced by Juraian energy. As soon as things settled down, he opened his eyes. His eyesight met with the top of Ranma's female form. Glancing down, he smiled.

"Did it work?" he asked. His voice taking on a tone more in tune with his new age.

Nodding, Ranma said, "Yes it did. However I have to tell you that you probably lost a couple hundred years because of what Misaki did."

Taking the new information in, Happi nodded.

Standing up, the two began their journey toward the Masaki household. Ranma plucked the furry cabbit off her shoulder and held it to her bosom. Gently she stroked Ryo-Ohki's head behind the ears in a special little spot. Ryo-Ohki purred in contentment. Somehow Ranma had found her favorite spot to be rubbed. A spot that very few humans could find. Only Sasami was ever able to find that spot.

"How do you feel? And why are you so tall?" asked Ranma.

Glancing over at the redhead, Happi smiled, "I feel great. I haven't felt this good in ages. And what do you mean why am I so tall? I was this tall on Jurai."

"You remember when we first encountered the NanBan Mirror and I was sent into the past. Well we saw you and Cologne. Only you two were much younger. Cologne was a little taller than I am but you were fairly short." said Ranma.

Nodding, Happosai said, "That's true. When Misaki sealed my access to the power of Jurai, she also forced it from my body. It was a bit painful. It had the effect of dehydrating my body of energy which resulted in my loss of height. My body compensated so it could survive on what miniscule power I had left. Human energy wasn't enough to return me to my original height though."

"Ah, I got it." said Ranma.

"So how about you? You didn't use up all your energy did you?" asked Happosai.

Shaking her head, Ranma said, "I'm not too bad. I have more energy left than I thought I would. My reserves must have grown last night when I stopped Nabiki from killing my mother."

"WHAT?" shouted Happosai as he twirled toward Ranma.

Realizing what she said out loud, Ranma said, "Shhh... Settle down. She's alright."

"What happened?" asked Happi. His face and tone serious. He cared a great deal for Nodoka. He didn't want to see anything happen to her.

"Last night while I was with Tsunami, I felt anger along my connection with the sword I made for mom using the light hawk wings. Someone was swinging the sword toward my mom's neck. I stopped the sword a few inches above her neck and then called out to her. She woke up and unmasked her attacker. It was Nabiki. Apparently Nabiki blames me for Akane's death. She thought she could kill my mother." said Ranma.

Sighing, Happi said, "I was afraid something like this would happen. That girl is smart but still, I can't believe she'd resort to something like this."

"Last night mom said that the death of a loved one can affect a person in many ways." said Ranma.

Nodding, Happosai said, "She is right. I just didn't expect it from Nabiki."

"Me neither" replied Ranma.

The two continued down the steps toward the Masaki household. Silence ensued as the two were lost to their own thoughts. Ranma however continued to pet Ryo-Ohki in that oh so special spot that most humans could never find.

Meanwhile...

Just outside the area that made up the valley where the Masaki shrine was built, a robed man walked along a path. The path would eventually lead him to the shrine itself. "Simple really. I just spliced in some Juraian DNA to your genetic structure. You're still yourself and all your genes are untouched, you just have some Juraian in you now. Which reminds me. Ranma, talk to your mother and see if it's possible for Cologne to use the Power of Jurai." said Washu.

While Cologne took the new information in, Ranma nodded, "Ok, I'll probably see her later today or tonight. I could link Cologne to my own power but I'd rather not have anything or anyone else linked to my power."

"You mean there is something that is tied to your own power?" asked a curious Washu.

"Just a sword I made with the light hawk wings for my mother before I left. I'm glad I made it for her too." said Ranma.

"A sword eh? An interesting use for the light hawk wings." said Washu, visions of experimentation on a sword dancing in her head.

Turning to Ranma, Cologne said, "As soon as I get used to my new body, I'll be able to begin your instruction as my heir. For now, I'd like to get back to the house and eat. It must be getting close to lunch time."

Gasping, Sasami said, "I almost forgot! I still have to make lunch!"

"Alright. Let's get back then. I am getting a bit hungry myself and we still need to introduce Cologne to Happosai." said Washu.

"Do you have anything I can wear besides these?" asked Cologne indicating the jeans and shirt she was clothed in.

Nodding, Washu asked, "What would you like?"

Thinking a moment, Cologne said, "Probably something like what Ranma is wearing."

Typing a few things into her terminal, Washu input Cologne's request. A moment later, Cologne was dressed in an outfit similar to what Ranma was wearing only the shirt was green instead of red.

Looking down at herself, Cologne nodded. "I can see why you wear these Ranma. They are very comfortable."

"Yeah they are. Anyways, let's get going. Sasami still has lunch to make and I'd like to help her make it." said Ranma.

"You mean it!" said an excited Sasami.

Smiling, Ranma nodded, "Yep. I can show you a few tricks too."

Crying with delight, Sasami hugged Ranma and began to drag him toward the subspace door to the house.

Chuckling, Cologne and Washu followed right behind them. Each smiling at how Ranma was being dragged along by the high spirited young girl.

Turning to Cologne, Washu said, "Oh, I almost forgot. Earlier when you were in the tube, I got a message that you have a visitor."

Curious, Cologne asked, "Do you know who it is?"

Shaking her head, Washu said, "No I don't. However we'll find out in a minute."

Nodding, Cologne said, "Alright, but there is something I have to give Happi first. Something that's been waiting three hundred years. And no, I won't tell you just yet. You'll have to wait like everyone else."

Nodding, Washu followed Ranma and Sasami out of her lab. Behind her, Cologne picked up her staff and joined the group leaving the lab.

On Ranma's shoulder, Ryo-Ohki simply enjoyed the ride.

Back in the Masaki household...

Katsuhito was curious about Mousse's martial arts style. He'd learnt about it when he visited the amazon village but from his knowledge, it was only used to hold small items.

"Mousse?" said Katsuhito.

"Yeah?" replied Mousse.

"I was curious as to your martial arts style. I've visited the amazon village in my travels and from what I know, the hidden weapons style was only used to hold small items. Yet Azaka and Kamidake said you had large amounts of weaponry and such on you." said Katsuhito.

"Well, that is true. The majority of the warriors that learn it use it to hold small items and weapons. However I am a master of it and can use it to carry large amounts of weapons on my body with relative ease." replied Mousse.

"How are you able to displace the weight? I've learned enough of it to be able to carry a bokken or sword but not much more." said Katsuhito.

"That's an amazon secret. Speaking of secrets, how do you know it?" said Mousse.

Chuckling, Katsuhito said, "Cologne taught me a long time ago."

"Oh, I suppose it's okay then. She is the Matriarch afterall. So how much longer do we have to wait?" said Mousse.

Before Katsuhito could speak, the door to Washu's lab opened. The three men sitting at the table turned toward the sound and watched as four people emerged.

Sasami appeared first, pulling a male Ranma toward the kitchen. Neither noticed the three men sitting at the table.

At the table, a chuckling Happosai said, "Only Ranma could get dragged around by a little girl."

As Sasami and Ranma quickly disappeared into the kitchen, Washu stepped out of the doorway next. Glancing around, she saw the three males sitting at the table watching her. Smiling, she turned back to the doorway and spoke to someone that none of the men could see.

"I wonder who she's talking to." said Happosai.

"I don't know. I don't remember seeing anyone other than Ranma and Sasami enter Washu's lab today." replied Katsuhito.

"I guess we'll find out in a moment then." said Happosai.

Mousse simply watched events unfold. He was curious who the girl that was dragging Ranma around. She appeared too young for him to be interested in. Then there was that red headed girl near the door that they had come out from. She was young but there was something about her that bothered him. Shrugging it off for the time being, he watched as the red head talked with someone that none of them could see yet.

Over at the door to Washu's lab, Washu whispered to Cologne, "Come on, it's time to make your grand debut."

Whispering back, Cologne said, "I can't believe I'm getting scared by something so simple. I mean what if he runs away from me. What if he treats me like Ranma treated my great granddaughter. What then?"

Sighing, Washu said quietly, "You've waited a long time for this. If you don't do it then you'll regret it. Maybe not today, and maybe not tomorrow, but soon and for the rest of your life. So get on out there and claim your man."

Cologne smiled as the young scientist used a quote from that old movie she'd seen back when movies were young. Who thought that those explorers would have something like that with them on an expedition.

Nodding slowly, Cologne began to step out into the view of the three men. Each reacted differently to the sight of her.

Katsuhito watched as a young woman came into view. He was curious as to who she was at first until he finally realized it was Cologne. Smiling, he wondered if her return to youth was because of Happosai's recent rejuvenation.

Mousse wondered why Shampoo was there. However before he spoke up, he took another look. No, it wasn't Shampoo but someone that looked very much like her. However there were several noticeable differences. Mainly the fact that the woman was at least twice Shampoo's age if not more. Mousse settled back down to watch and see what happened next.

Happosai however recognized her immediately. He'd never forget that vision of loveliness as long as he lived. She was the first woman he fell in love with on earth. The woman that gave him a chance only to have him ruin any hope for happiness because of his petty reasons. Well, no more would he be like he was in the past. He swore that he'd never do any of that again and he'd abide by that oath. Somehow Cologne had regained her youth and he was going to make it up to her for what he put her though all those years ago.

Over by the door to Washu's lab, Cologne watched as each male reacted to her emergence. She could see curiosity in Katsuhito, probably wondering who she was before a look of recognition flashed in his eyes. That irritated her a little but she'd let it slide for now since he seemed to finally remember. The next was Mousse. Wondering why he was here, she watched as he seemed to recognize her only to settle back down as if realizing she wasn't someone else. *Probably thought I was Xian Pu. I'll find out what he's doing here after I finish my business with Happi.* she thought.

Finally she looked at Happosai and smiled as she saw the look of recognition in his eyes. When he smiled back, her heart soared. He remembered her! She watched as several emotions played across his face. Love, sadness, shame, resolve, and finally settling on love again. He still loved her, after all these years. Her worries forgotten, she began her short journey toward Happosai.

As Cologne moved toward the dining table, Happosai stood and began moving toward Cologne. Soon they stood only a couple feet apart. Each gazed into the other's eyes for a moment that seemed to stretch on into infinity while the outside world faded. Finally, one of them spoke.

"Is that really you Kho-chan?" asked Happosai.

Smiling, Cologne nodded, "Yes it's me Happi."

"How?" asked Happi.

"Washu was kind enough to give me my youth back." replied Cologne.

"What made you do this?" asked Happi.

Smirking, Cologne stepped closer to Happosai and replied, "Do you really want to know?"

"Yes I do." replied Happosai, his voice slightly softer.

"Do you remember what happened between you and I in my village three hundred years ago?" asked Cologne with a predatory smirk.

"Um... a little bit." replied Happosai.

Moving closer, Cologne wrapped her arms around his neck. She could hear his heartbeat speeding up and his nervousness increase. Smiling to herself, she decided to proceed with her plans.

"Let me refresh your memory." she said before pulling him into a passionate kiss.

Happosai's arms flailed about a minute in the suddenness of the kiss he was receiving. However soon he calmed down and wrapped his arms around Cologne's youthened body.

Cologne smiled internally. Everything was right with her world.

Over by the lab door, Washu smiled at the touching scene. It filled her heart with happiness to have helped two people come together. Later she'd see about getting Happosai in for some tests. She was still a bit curious as to how a Juraian could survive off human life energy.

At the table, Katsuhito smiled as well. He was happy for Cologne. He remembered back when he first met Cologne. At the time she was sad because her heart had been broken. He tried to help her as best he could over the years but her heart never got over Happosai. Sure she eventually married another, but a part of her heart always belonged to Happosai.

With a sigh, Mousse simply watched the scene as it played out. He had a good guess as to who the woman was now. He'd heard Happosai call her Kho-chan. His shock soon turned to curiosity as to how someone could go from looking like a dried up mummy to looking like a young woman. He'd make sure to ask about it later once Cologne and Happosai parted. He smiled as he watched the two. Briefly he wondered when and if he'd ever find someone.

To him, Cologne might have been a hard person to work for, as well as live with, but she was human and she deserved to find some personal happiness. However the comment about aging was nagging him. He added that to his list of things to ask about later.

Perhaps she will give up on finding Ranma, but until then she will have to realize that she cannot get everything she wants. For now, I am happy to see you."

Stepping out of the kitchen, Sasami said, "I'll get it."

Smiling, Sasami said, "Yes? May I help you?"

Returning the smile, Nodoka said, "I hope so. I was wondering if this was the Masaki shrine?"

Shaking her head, Sasami replied, "No, this is the Masaki household. The shrine is up the stairs over by the sacred tree."

"I'm so glad your alright. I was afraid I might lose you." said Ranma into his mother's kimono.

"I'm ok. I have you to thank. If it were not for the sword you made, I would not be here now." replied Nodoka.

Nodding, Nodoka followed Ranma into the house. Kasumi, Ukyo, and Sasami close behind.
