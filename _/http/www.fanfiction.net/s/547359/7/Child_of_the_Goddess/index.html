Child of the Goddess

Chapter 6

** = Thoughts

It was early evening at the Masaki shrine. Things were relatively quiet despite the earlier explosion which rocked the shrine. The birds were singing and the crickets were emerging as dusk drew nearer. It was really quite tranquil. Just as a shrine should be.

Inside the shrine sat six people. Three residents and three visitors. Latched onto one of the visitors was Sasami, the energetic young lady of the shrine. She appeared very happy and was content to hug the girl with the mixed hair color.

Cologne watched and simply chuckled. *Leave it to Ranma to have girls throwing themselves at him. But I wonder who Rhea is? Hmm...*

Katsuhito simply watched Ranma try to deal with Sasami's vice like grip. *So this is Tsunami's child. But I wonder how Sasami knew her name? Hmm...*

Happi smiled and wondered if there was any way he could get girls to throw themselves at him like that. He envied his newest student for that ability.

Washu's brain was trying to figure out how this girl got here without her noticing. Add to the fact that the girl was wearing the same clothes Ranma had on. The only thing she could come up with was that the girl was Ranma. But Ranma was male, wasn't he?

Looking around at everyone, Ranma sheepishly rubbed the back of her head. Turning her attention to Washu, he said, "I'm Ranma."

Blinking, Washu asked, "But I thought Ranma was male."

"I am." replied Ranma.

"Then why are you female?" asked a still perplexed Washu. This would require full body metamorphosis, similarly to how Ryo-Ohki transformed into a ship.

Sighing, Ranma said, "It's a curse. While I was in China, me and Genma, my former father, visited a training ground called Jusenkyo. It's filled with cursed springs and if you fall in one you take on the form of what drowned there last. I fell into the spring of drowned girl. Now cold water turns me into a female while hot water turns me back into a male."

Nodding, Washu filed the name away for later. She'd have to look into this Jusenkyo place. If there was one thing she loved, it was figuring out a mystery. Returning to the subject at hand, she asked, "Ok, I can accept that. So why did Sasami call you Rhea?"

Blinking at someone so accepting of his curse, Ranma looked around, unsure of what to say to these people. Glancing over at Cologne, she saw her nod that it was ok to tell them. Nodding back, she returned her attention to the red head.

"Well, in my past life, that was my name. I was born on a planet called Jurai to a woman named..." he began before being interrupted.

"Tsunami" said Washu.

Nodding, Ranma asked, "Yes. How did you know?"

Smiling, she said, "Oh that's easy. You see, Katsuhito and Sasami here are Jurian. We all know who Tsunami is."

Blinking a moment, Ranma smiled, "That's great! Can you tell me where I can find her?"

Turning to Katsuhito, Washu asked, "Would you?"

Nodding, the old priest cleared his throat, "That is a complex story. But listen and know that I speak the truth."

Seeing her nod, Katsuhito began, "Long ago, Washu's daughter was controlled by an evil man named Kagato. One day, she attacked Jurai. Inside the palace, a very young girl was visiting the royal garden where many of the Jurian trees resided. In one of the attacks, she was thrown from a platform and fell deep into the garden. There she would have died had it not been for where she landed. She landed next to the tree Tsunami. Tsunami appeared and merged with the girl to save her life."

A little dejected, Ranma said, "So that means I can't find her?"

Smiling, Katsuhito replied, "Not at all. The girl I mentioned happens to be none other than Sasami."

"You mean?" asked Ranma, a little more excited than before.

Nodding, the priest said, "Yes. Tsunami and Sasami will eventually merge into one being. Tsunami has said that they will be both mother and sister to you. However, I believe that Sasami can take you to meet your mother so that she can explain better than I. "

Absorbing the new information, Ranma looked down at the little bundle in her arms. Softly she spoke, more to herself than any other, "A sister? I always wondered what it would be like to have a sister." Running her hand through Sasami's hair, Ranma smiled.

Looking up at Ranma, Sasami smiled. She heard what Ranma said. About the curse and her words about having a sister. Renewing the hug, she asked, "So do you want to be my oneeniichan?" (Oneeniichan means Big Brother/Sister)

Returning the hug, Ranma smiled and nodded. Seeing the joy in Sasami's eyes made Ranma think to herself, *She's really kind and innocent. I think I'm going to like having a little sister. I just wonder how I'm going to explain her to mom."

The others simply watched the touching moment as Ranma gained a little sister while Sasami gained a big brother and a big sister all in one.

After the hug was complete, Ranma repositioned Sasami so she was sitting in her lap. Sasami leaned back and made herself comfortable before further talks could resume. She smiled now that she had found her oneeniichan.

Looking up at the others, Ranma realized she was the center of attention as everyone was smiling at the two. Blushing a little, she sheepishly rubbed the back of her head. "So... umm... what now?" she asked.

It was about that time that another explosion rocked the shrine. A few books fell along with a some other knickknacks. Once it had subsided, Ranma asked, "What's with the explosions?"

Looking over at Ranma, Katsuhito said, "Currently we have a few others that are staying here. All of them are from offworld. The explosions are caused by two of them fighting with each other. Ryoko, Washu's daughter, and Ayeka, my sister, are always fighting over my grandson."

Blinking a moment, Ranma asked, "He's not engaged to them is he?"

Shaking his head, Katsuhito replied, "No, although I believe that Ryoko and Ayeka would like nothing better than to be married to him. Why do you ask?"

Chuckling, Cologne interrupted Ranma before she could respond, "Probably because Ranma here had several girls chasing after him who would fight with each other over him. Then there were the guys that were after the girls who were after him... It didn't help matters much that all of the girls were engaged to him as well!"

"How did you get engaged to so many girls, all at once? I didn't think polygamy was widely practiced on this planet." asked Katsuhito.

His visage turning a little darker, Ranma responded, "It was my old man's fault. You see, he isn't my father by blood. I only just recently learned that. However when I was real young he took me on a ten year martial arts training trip. During our travels, he would constantly engage me to various girls for food, money, or whatever he needed. The worst one I think was when he traded me for a bowl of rice, a fish, and two pickles."

"That's awful!" cried Sasami as she hugged her oneeniichan.

Looking down at Sasami, Ranma smiled and ran his fingers through her hair. "It's alright, Imouto-chan. I've managed to get out of most of them already. There's only one or two left, plus whatever ones I don't know of to deal with."

"I agree with Sasami on this one, Ranma. What kind of person uses their child as a commodity?" interjected Washu.

Looking over at Washu, Ranma simply said, "One that will learn the error of his ways." Receiving a confused look, Ranma added, "Don't worry about it for now."

Nodding, Washu turned to regard the others and their reaction to this latest tidbit.

Cologne and Happi seemed the most calm as they had heard this before.

Katsuhito, however seemed to develop a hardness in his eyes. What it was she couldn't ascertain but she believed that if Ranma's former father and Katsuhito met, then the man that used Ranma like that would not walk away alive.

Just hearing about that brought back somewhat painful memories of her own son. The one she lost so long ago. Looking over at Ranma, she studied him a little better than before. *Hmm, perhaps that's why he seemed familiar. He resembles my own lost son a little bit. Sigh. I wonder whatever happened to my son. It's been so long that I sometimes wonder if any of his descendants are still around.*

She was broken from her musings by yet another shrine shaking explosion. Shaking her head, she mumbled something about experimental behavior modification and two new test subjects.

Katsuhito glanced over at Ranma, "Ranma?"

"Yes?" he replied.

"I was wondering if you would go and see if you can stop those two from making so much noise and destroying everything?" asked the old priest.

"Um... Well... Sure, I can if you want. But why me?" replied Ranma.

"Well, I can tell by the way you walk and hold yourself that you know quite a bit about martial arts. I was thinking that you might like a little workout before supper." said Katsuhito.

Nodding, Ranma stood up along with Sasami. Looking down at her, Ranma asked, "Can you show me the way?"

Smiling, Sasami nodded and said, "Of course I can. I have to go and start on dinner anyway."

"Ok then. Let's go." she replied.

Just before they left, Katsuhito spoke up, "Ranma?"

Turning, she replied, "Yes?"

"When you get there, tell them that you are there for Tenchi. They will know what I'm talking about." spoke the old man.

"Um... sure..." replied a confused Ranma. Shrugging, she left with Sasami on her shoulders.

*******************

Back inside the shrine, Washu sat chuckling away. "Good one, Katsuhito! I'd love to see what happens when she says that to them!"

Curious, the two elders asked, "What's so funny?"

Before Katsuhito could respond, Washu answered, "Because, those two are fiercely competitive over Tenchi's heart. If Ranma says that to them then they will think she's after Tenchi. Oh, I have so got to see this."

Turning to Katsuhito, Cologne asked, "Why did you do that?"

"Because I could see in Ranma's eyes that something was troubling her. I thought that a good fight might help get her mind off things for a while. Besides, I think she will do fine. Ryoko and Ayeka are powerful but they won't kill her, and if my guess is right, Ranma should be able to hold her own against them. Don't worry." responded Katsuhito.

Shaking her head, Cologne said, "I am not worried about Ranma. She can handle things that I could only dream of, but with what happened a couple weeks ago, I don't think she'll respond lightly to someone saying she's after someone's heart. What you saw was most likely her still grieving over the loss of the fiancee that won her heart."

"What did happen? I was going to ask but I forgot about it with the whole changing of genders and other stuff." said Washu.

Looking at each of the three remaining people, Cologne spoke, "Ranma was battling the phoenix king Saffron. He was trying to save the life of Akane, his main fiancee. However Saffron unleashed a final attack meant to kill Ranma and the rest of us that were there. I believe it was during those final moments that Ranma's power awoke. He stopped Saffron's blast with the Wings of the Light Hawk. Then he did something. I'm still not entirely sure how he did it. All I know is that Akane was dead and Ranma was very angry with Saffron."

"What did he do?" inquired Washu.

"Well, from what I know, the Wings are a tool. They can be called forth to be used and then dispelled." she said.

Seeing everyone nodding, Cologne continued, "Well, he changed them. He somehow combined the Wings with another technique that he was taught."

"Which technique would that be?" asked Katsuhito.

"The attack was called Wings of the Cats Claws. An odd name but quite suitable for the attack. The technique he combined with the wings was the Neko-Ken." replied Cologne.

Washu noticed Katsuhito's eyes take on an icy steel appearance. He knew whatever this Neko-ken was. She'd have to look into it later. For now, she was more curious as to what happened with the wings. One of the few mysteries she was still trying to figure out.

"What happened to the wings then?" she asked.

Turning to Washu, Cologne said, "Well, the tips turned a midnight black. So black that it was as if no light could escape from them. Then, claws burst forth as though they were ripping themselves out of a fleshy prison. The attack encircled the phoenix king and eventually he was killed. But what troubled me the most was that the Phoenix king wasn't reborn from his ashes. Whatever Ranma did, destroyed the process of resurrection."

Absorbing this a moment, Washu asked, "Alright, so what is this Neko-Ken you spoke of?"

Before Cologne could speak, Katsuhito interrupted, "It is a ancient martial arts technique. Nobody knows how old it truly is. However for very many years it has been banned. This is due to the danger of it."

"How dangerous could it be?" Washu asked.

Cologne interjected at this point, "The training is very dangerous. Not only to the student but the teacher as well. When the student is very young, the teacher will wrap them head to toe in fish sausages or various other fish products. Then they will toss the student into a pit of starving cats. Repeat until the student is dead or has learnt the cat fist. If the student learns the cat fist then they will most likely attack the teacher. Afterwards, the student will either be stuck in a feral state or somehow manage to come out of it. Ranma is the only one I have ever heard of coming out of it."

Washu's face had paled as Cologne described the Neko-Ken. "Who would do such a thing to a child?" she loudly whispered.

"His Father. Or at least the person he used to call father. The man was an idiot in most things. However when it came to martial arts he was an effective teacher. But he lacked common sense when it came to teaching Ranma new techniques. I don't know much more. Only Ranma knows fully what Genma put him through." said Cologne.

"Perhaps we all should follow and watch to make sure Ranma doesn't hurt them too bad." stated Happi before taking another puff of his pipe.

Receiving nods all around, the four stood up and stepped out into the courtyard intent on following Ranma.

*******************

Meanwhile...

Ranma and Sasami finished leaping down the stairs. Sasami was giggling and enjoying the wonderful ride her oneeniichan had given her. She loved it when they leapt high into the air where she could see all around.

Arriving at the house, Ranma set Sasami down and smiled at her. "Did you enjoy yourself?" he asked.

Nodding happily, Sasami hugged him. "That was so fun! I wanna do it again!"

Hugging her back, Ranma smiled and nodded, "Alright, but not tonight. Now, can you tell me where to find this Ayeka and Ryoko?"

Thinking a moment, Sasami nodded. Grabbing Ranma's hand, she pulled her over to the sliding doors. Sliding them open, she pulled Ranma inside.

Ryoko and Ayeka were playing tug of war with Tenchi as the rope. When the doors slid open, they stopped a moment to look up and see who entered. What greeted them was a young woman with red and silver blue hair. A mixed eye color and slitted pupils.

"I was told I could find you here." she said suspecting that the two girls fighting over the guy were Ayeka and Ryoko.

Ayeka, Ryoko, and Tenchi didn't know who she was talking to. Before they could respond, Ranma said what the old priest told her to say. "I was told to tell you that I'm here for Tenchi."

Now this got a fast reaction out of the two girls. Each jumped up and called forth their power.

Ryoko ignited her energy blade and snarled at Ranma. "I don't know who you think you are but nobody takes my Tenchi away from me."

Ayeka narrowed her eyes at the newcomer. Calling forth her logs, she spoke, "For once I agree with Ryoko on something. However he's not yours you monster woman!"

"Stuff it princess. We can deal with that later. For now, let's take care of this tramp." replied Ryoko.

Ranma narrowed her eyes as she thought she had just been set up. If her guess was correct, then Tenchi was the name of the boy. Shuddering at the thought that she just stated she was here for him, she backed out of the house as the two girls approached. Well, she shoved her foot in her mouth, might as well have some fun with it.

Following Ranma out, Ryoko and Ayeka attacked. Flying at Ranma, Ryoko swung her sword only to cut through air. Looking around, she couldn't find a trace of the tramp that was trying to steal her Tenchi. Suddenly, someone landed on her. Looking over her shoulder, Ryoko saw it was Ranma. Snarling, she teleported away. Ranma, losing her perch, fell to the ground.

Ranma landed on the ground only to be attacked by Ayeka. Seeing logs surround her, Ranma wondered what they were for a moment before they activated. Arcs of electricity jumped from log to log as well as to her body. Looking down, she wondered what the light show was supposed to do. It didn't bother her in the least. In fact it was kind of enjoyable. Almost pleasurable. Looking up, she saw the shocked expression on Ayeka's face.

"You know, you really need to learn something besides this little light show." Ranma said knowing it would infuriate the girl.

Fuming, Ayeka dispelled the logs. "Azaka! Kamidake!"

"Yes Mistress." they both replied as they appeared.

"Attack that girl!" she commanded.

"At once your highness." they responded, rushing forward.

Ranma simply smiled as she brought out her claws and made them visible. Apparently Ayeka and Ryoko were a little surprised by their appearance. "Ooh, scratching posts. You are too kind." Ranma said letting her more cat like personality come out to play.

Seeing the girl. The girl with the feral cat like look. The feral cat like girl with the glowing claws. Those sharp, sharp, wood slicing claws. The two guardians decided that perhaps today was not a good day to die. Turning, they floated back behind the princess. "Were sorry your highness, but we weren't programmed to be scratching posts."

Up above Ryoko was laughing. "Some guardians princess! Running away from a little girl!

Looking up, Ayeka yelled, "Oh shut up you monster woman!"

Looking skyward, Ranma smiled and said, "Hmm, You don't look like a monster to me. More like a Tomboy."

That got Ryoko riled up. She prided herself on her appearance. Glaring down at the girl that dared to make fun of her, she summoned her power. Her eyes began to glow a crimson red as power flowed to her hand. Once ready, she threw the energy ball at the red/silver-blue headed girl.

Ranma simply stood there examining Ryoko for a moment. She didn't know why she used that insult but it seemed appropriate. The blast that was headed for her was powerful. But it lacked the finesse of her own. One thing was clear though. This Ryoko was powerful. *Hmm, I wonder if she can take hand to hand as well as she can use ki blasts. Let's see.*

Ryoko was unprepared as Ranma leapt over her energy ball to attack her. Behind Ranma the ball impacted and exploded causing Ayeka to put up her shield. Ranma started things off with a few basic punches to gauge Ryoko's reflexes. Ranma wasn't disappointed as Ryoko blocked each one.

As Ranma and Ryoko began to turn up the heat, Ayeka was in self contemplation. *Why wasn't that girl affected by my attack? It's never failed me before.*

"Azaka. Kamidake. Tell me why my attack didn't affect that girl." commanded Ayeka.

"Scanning..." said the two logs.

Up above, Ryoko and Ranma traded punches back and forth continually speeding up. Ranma couldn't fly but she was jumping to reach Ryoko. Ranma was impressed that the girl could keep up with her without having to resort to the Amaguriken. An added plus was that Ryoko seemed to be able to take punches as well as Ryoga. She smiled at that. She'd just found herself a good sparring partner. Plus, due to the joining, she didn't have as much qualms of hitting girls as before. She didn't like it if the girl was weaker than her, but this girl could take it and give it back.

*******************

Down below...

"Scan complete princess." responded the twin totems.

"Alright then. Now tell me why my attack wouldn't work on that girl." commanded Ayeka.

"Unknown. The girl appears to be Jurian but her power is undetectable." said Azaka.

"Conjecture would indicated that the girl is unaffected by any Jurian power." said Kamidake.

Thinking to herself, Ayeka thought, *That's odd. Juraians should be affected by the power of Jurai. If that girl is Jurian, and the power of Jurai cannot affect her, then that must mean she is somehow able to control Tsunami's power. Then that must mean... Oh no! I've just attacked Tsunami's child!*

Off to the side, the four from the shrine just arrived to see Ayeka look up in shock. Seeing her look over at them, Washu and Katsuhito simply nodded. Looking up, they could see Ranma having fun with Ryoko.

Washu had her computer out analyzing the fight. Occasionally her eyes would bug out as to how much damage Ranma would be doing if this was anyone but Ryoko.

Cologne and Happi simply watched and smiled. It was nice that Ranma was able to find a new sparring partner. Someone that could give as well as take.

Katsuhito watched Ranma as he moved from style to style seamlessly. *So I was right. He is as good as I thought. No, better. Hmm... I wonder if he'd like to train with Tenchi some. Tenchi could use some experience with an unarmed opponent. And perhaps Ranma would like to learn Jurian sword techniques. She appears to use only unarmed techniques.*

Ayeka had walked over to the others to watch the fight. Looking up, she saw a smile on her older brothers face. "You put that girl up to it? Didn't you?"

"Why whatever could you mean Ayeka?" he replied as though he didn't know what she was talking about.

"You know very well what I'm talking about. You knew that was Tsunami's child and you told her to say that she was here for Tenchi." said Ayeka.

"I seem to recall saying something like that. But at my age I cannot recall everything I say." he replied.

The elders chuckled at this. They too loved to play these little games with youngsters. Helped to keep young people on their toes.

Back at the battle, Ryoko was getting furious. Here she was trying to stop this floozy from taking her Tenchi away and she had yet to hurt the girl. The girl just floated through her attacks. It was like trying to hit water or air. It didn't help matters either that the girl kept critiquing her moves.

"Don't overextend yourself."

"Keep your guard up."

"You'll get more power if you keep your leg forward."

Ryoko was getting about fed up with this. All her attacks either were blocked or dodged. Those that did land were not even slowing the girl down. It was time for the last attack. Gathering all her power, she prepared to unleash a large and very powerful ki attack. She was so caught up in her fury, that she didn't realize there were others in the line of her attack.

Ranma however saw what was about to happen and who was in the line of destruction. If she didn't act fast, there were going to be some people hurt. Quickly she ran so she was just in front of everyone. Ryoko unleashed her attack. The ball of ki rushed toward them kicking dirt up as it passed near.

Ranma held up her hands and called forth her power. Ten wings of light appeared just before the attack reached them. The attack was very powerful but Ranma was able to stop it and allow it to dissipate.

After unleashing her attack, Ryoko realized there were people there. She realized that she had unintentionally done something she swore she'd never to do again. She swore she'd never hurt another innocent. And here she was just about to kill innocent bystanders. What made it worse was it was her adopted family.

Just as she was about to race forward, ten wings of light appeared to stop her attack. She just floated there dumbfounded as her attack died off. There, behind the disappearing wings was the girl she was fighting. She now realized who the girl was. The girl was Tsunami's child. Seeing Katsuhito behind the girl with a couple of lawn gnomes and her mother, Ryoko could guess that the girl said she was there for Tenchi cause the old man put her up to it.

Ranma stood there sweating. It had been a good workout but using the wings had drained her more than she thought. She remembered long ago that her mother told her she'd have to practice so her power would grow stronger and that she'd be able to use it without wearing herself out. Probably the only reason she didn't suffer as much during Saffron was because she was too angry to pass out. Her anger had given her enough reserve power to last until they setup camp that night.

Her power drained, Ranma slumped forward into unconsciousness.

Sasami, just coming out to get everyone for dinner, saw Ranma fall unconscious. Seeing this, she ran forward crying. "Oneeniichan!" she yelled as she ran. The others turned to watch her run up and kneel down next to Ranma.

"Oneeniichan! Oneeniichan! Wake up!" she cried.

Washu stepped forward and knelt down. "It's alright Sasami. Ranma is just tired from using her power. She will be alright."

Drying her tears, Sasami nodded to Washu. "You sure?"

Nodding, Washu smiled. "Yes, I'm sure."

Katsuhito bent down and picked Ranma up. "Let us all go inside. I will tell you what is going on while Ranma here rests."

Seeing nods all around, everyone went back inside. Some to hear explanations. Others to eat. And others to be reprimanded.

Washu and Ryoko hung back. "What you did was reckless Ryoko. You could have easily killed someone."

"I know. I didn't mean to but that girl just got me so riled up. More so than Ayeka does." replied Ryoko.

"It's alright. Katsuhito put her up to saying that about Tenchi." said Washu.

"I thought as much. I just hope she doesn't hold it against me." responded Ryoko.

Smiling, Washu said, "I don't think that will be a problem. I don't know if you noticed, but Ranma was smiling while she was fighting you. I think she was enjoying it up till the end there."

"You may be right. I've never met anyone that could keep up with me like that." chuckled Ryoko.

"Yes, well. She's been learning how to fight most of her life. I think even Katsuhito was impressed. I wouldn't put it past him to try and get Ranma and Tenchi to spar together." said Washu.

"I don't mind that. Just so long as she's not after my Tenchi." replied Ryoko.

Washu chuckled, "I wouldn't worry about that. I believe, despite appearances, Ranma isn't interested in men."

"You mean?" said a wide eyes Ryoko.

"Get your mind outta the gutter Ryoko. Ranma is under a curse. She was born male and turns female when she is splashed by cold water. Hot water returns her to male form. She told us where she acquired it and I am going to look into it. I'm curious as to how it works." said Washu.

"Oh. So that's why Sasami was calling her oneeniichan. Are you going to try to figure out how to cure it?" asked a curious Ryoko.

Shaking her head, Washu said, "It'll probably come up but I don't believe Ranma will want to be cured. Ranma is Tsunami's child. He's been both male and female and if my guess is correct, the curse doesn't bother him too much. In fact I believe he's accepted it. So no, I won't offer to cure it." replied Washu.

Nodding, Ryoko followed Washu into the house. Sitting around the table was everyone except Ranma whom was upstairs resting after the ordeal outside.

Katsuhito motioned everyone to be quiet. Once everyone was quiet, he began to explain what was going on. Who Ranma was and what he had learnt earlier with the exception of the Neko-Ken. How Ranma was Tsunami's child and the new sibling relationship between Ranma and Sasami. Cologne and Happi interjected when needed. While explanations were going on, everyone was served dinner. Ryoko apologized to everyone. Especially the four that were in the way of her attack. But most of all, Ryoko apologized to Sasami for trying to hurt Ranma.

*******************

As all of this was going on, upstairs Ranma slept. A light purple glow surrounded her body. Her hair changed so it was more of a 50/50 mix of red and silver blue and her incisors lengthened slightly to be more cat-like. As the newest changes finished, the glow subsided.

*******************

Downstairs...

"I'm going upstairs to check on Rhea." said Sasami with Ryo-Ohki in her arms. Stepping over to the stairs, she looked back to see everyone still talking. With no one paying attention, she climbed the stairs one by one.

As she neared the top of the stairs, she whispered to Ryo-Ohki, "I hope Rhea is ok. I know Washu said she would be but I'm still worried about her."

"Miyahh" replied her furry companion.

Opening the door to where her oneeniichan rested, she failed to see the purple glow as it finished subsiding. Closing the door behind her, she stepped over and knelt next to Ranma's female body. Ryo-Ohki hopped down and sat on Ranma's belly, watching the girl as she slept.

"Ryo-Ohki?" said Sasami.

Looking up at her favorite person, Ryo-Ohki Miyahh'd. "Miyahh?"

Smiling down at her furry friend, Sasami responded, "I'm going to take Rhea to meet Tsunami. Stay here and keep an eye on us, ok?"

Nodding, Ryo-Ohki Miyahh'd.

Laying down next to Ranma's body, Sasami cuddled up to her. Once comfortable, marks of power flared on her forehead before she succumbed to her induced slumber.

Ryo-Ohki simply watched from her perch on Ranma's belly. She was worried but knew that Sasami would be ok. Looking over at the face of her perch, Ryo-Ohki couldn't help but be drawn to this new arrival. This new arrival felt special in some way to her. She hoped the new person would be her friend.

*******************

In a dimensional plain...

Drip...

Drip...

Drip...

"Yes dear. Once I awoke, I found the sword near my neck and I rolled out of the way to one of the walls. When I looked into the room, I saw the sword floating there while my attacker on the other side of it. My son told me I was in danger so I grabbed the sword just before my attacker tried to escape. Fortunately I was able to stop them at the door. It was there I unmasked them." said Nodoka.

"So who attacked you?" asked Ukyo.

"Yes Auntie. Who was it?" asked Kasumi.

Sighing, Nodoka took a long sip of her tea. Looking at the two girls, she felt terrible for having to do this. Especially to Kasumi.

"Kasumi dear. I'm afraid you will not like what I'm about to say." said Nodoka.

Slightly worried, Kasumi asked, "What do you mean Auntie?"

"Dear, the person who attacked me was Nabiki" said Nodoka.

"Oh My. Why would Nabiki do such a thing?" said Kasumi, her voice obviously alarmed.

"Before she left, she turned back and told me she'd have her revenge for Akane. She believes Ranma allowed Akane to die." said Nodoka.

A few tears began to make their way down Kasumi's Face. Ukyo scooted closer and wrapped the girl in a hug. That was enough for Kasumi to bury her head into the young chef's shoulder and begin to cry.

"Shhh... It's alright sugar. Just let it all out." said Ukyo.

"I'm afraid I have more to tell you two." said Nodoka.

Looking over at Nodoka, Ukyo asked, "What more is there?"

"Ranma wanted me to bring Kasumi here for tonight so we'd all be safe. Tomorrow he wanted all three of us to join him at where he is staying." said Nodoka.

Sniffling, Kasumi looked up, "You mean Ranma-kun doesn't blame me for Nabiki's behavior?"

"No dear. Whatever made you think that?" asked Nodoka.

Bowing her head, Kasumi said, "I should have seen what was happening. It's my fault that I didn't see that Nabiki blamed Ranma-kun for Akane's death."

Reaching across the table, Nodoka took Kasumi's hands in her own. Kasumi looked up to see Nodoka smiling at her. "It's not your fault dear. I suspected she blamed Ranma and was going to attempt something. Just remember that she made her own choice. It's not your fault."

Nodding a little, Kasumi sniffled again. "Where's Nabiki now?" she asked.

"I don't know dear. She ran out of the house after I unmasked her. Before I got you, I left a note for your father and Genma about what happened. I don't believe she'll return to the house but my son wanted to make sure we were safe." said Nodoka.

"That's so kind of him to think of my safety." said Kasumi.

"Me too sugar. I still can't believe Nabiki would do something like that. It's just not like her." said Ukyo.

"I don't know either. She seemed fine when I told her dinner was ready." said Kasumi.

"I believe we will all feel much better after some sleep. Tomorrow we can set out to where my son is staying." said Nodoka.

"What about Nabiki? What if she tries again?" asked Ukyo.

"Don't worry dear. My son said the sword would protect us. That he'd make sure of it. However I do believe we should take some extra precautions before turning in. Lock the doors and windows. Make sure things are secure. That sort of thing." said Nodoka.

"Don't worry about that. This place is pretty secure. You live in this area long enough and you'll invest in materials that are stronger than normal." said Ukyo.

"Very Well. Now why don't we all turn in. It's been a long night and we will need our strength for tomorrow." said Nodoka.

Receiving nods all around, the three ladies traveled upstairs where they secured the door. Ukyo pulled out two extra futons for Nodoka and Kasumi to sleep on. The three laid down and prepared to sleep. Tomorrow was going to be a busy day.

*******************

Ranma smiled at his mother. She just explained what that lady Misaki did and how to undo it. Tomorrow he'd talk to Happosai about it.

Feeling something along the link to the sword, Ranma closed his eyes and checked it. A moment later he opened his eyes and smiled.

"They made it." he said.

Smiling, Tsunami said, "That is good. I am happy my co-mother is safe."

Ranma simply nodded.

"Now son, would you like to continue or would you like to return to your body and rest?" asked Tsunami.

"Um... what about Sasami?" asked Ranma.

Smiling, Tsunami said, "She is fine. Currently she is sleeping. The link can be maintained until I release it."

Nodding, Ranma closed his eyes and concentrated. His body flared briefly before it calmed down and his female form stood there.

Looking up at her mother, Ranma said, "Um... If it's alright with you, I'd like to rest, but... well... I was wondering if we could rest together. Sorta a sleepover kind of thing."

Blinking, Tsunami smiled. Waving her hand, a plush bed grew from the water. Stepping over to it, she sat upon it. Ranma joined her shortly after. They both lay down facing one another.

Yawning, Ranma said, "This training tired me out."

Smiling, Tsunami said, "Yes, it will for the time being as you begin to use your power more and more. However eventually you will be able to use it all you want and not tire as much."

Closing her eyes, Ranma said, "That's good to know."

Tsunami pulled her child into herself so that the two were cuddled up together. Ranma resisted slightly at first but relented and soon snuggled deeper into her mother's embrace.

Half asleep, Ranma muttered, "I love you mom."

Leaning down, Tsunami kissed her child's forehead. "I love you too."

Soon the two settled down and fell asleep. Mother and sometimes daughter cuddled up and rested after a eventful time of training and saving others.

A young amazon female sat in her room brooding. She wasn't able to find any sign of her airen today. However there was always tomorrow. He couldn't escape forever. And when she found him, she would show him once and for all his place.

Yes, tomorrow she would begin anew.
