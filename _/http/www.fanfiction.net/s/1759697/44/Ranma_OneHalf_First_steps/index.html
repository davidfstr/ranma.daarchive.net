"Have you considered just what kind of ceremony we'd have?"

Naota blinked and scratched his head in thought.

"Ah, no, actually." he said in response to Lias question. "I never really thought about it. But now that you mention it, I think I'd like a nice, simple wedding. Just friends and family."

"So would I." Lia said as she leaned against Naota. "But I meant what faith would it be? I mean, I'm Shinto -Buddhist and you're Catholic, despite whats happened recently."

"Oh." Naota said. "Well, we could have a double ceremony. One Buddhist, one Christian."

"That'll work." Lia said. Arcing an eyebrow she looked at Naota. "So you really want to get married?"

Naota shrugged. "Well, since we're already mated, I figured we might as well make it official." he said.

"So when do you want to do it?" Lia asked.

"In the spring? After we graduate?" Naota suggested. "That way we can have the ceremony and not have to worry about gate -crashers from school."

"Okay." Lia said as she snuggled closer to Naota.

"Well, lets go tell your parents." Naota said as La tightened her grip on his arm.

"Can it wait? I'm sleepy and theres a sunbeam on us..." Lia mewed.

Naota yawned as he realized that he was due for a nap as well, and the patch of sun they were in was just so inviting.

"Okay." he yawned as he leaned back against the wall.

As Naota and Lia were drifting off in the dojo Akane was finishing reading the paper. Finishing an article on some monsters rampage in Jubaan Akane looked up and was surprised to find the house dead silent.

Looking up at the clock she realized why the house was so silent: it was ten AM, or as it was more commonly known around the Saotome household, nap time.

"I've been living with three Neko -ken influenced people for how long and I forget about nap time?" Akane thought as she got up for some fresh tea.

The sudden ringing of the phone startled Akane as she waited while her tea steeped. She decided to let the machine pick it up and half listened as the message played before the beep. When Nabikis voice came on Akane hurried to the phone and picked it up.

"Nabiki?" Akane said after she pressed the 'talk' button.

"Hey Akane." Nabiki answered. "I catch you at a bad time?"

"Nah." Akane answered as she headed back into the kitchen. "I was just making some tea."

"So whats up?" Nabiki asked. "Anything new happening in Nerima?'

"Well..." Akane said as she picked up her mug. "You know that boy that Lia was engaged to?"

"Yeah." Nabiki answered. "What was his name? Naoya?"

'Naota." corrected Akane.

"Naota." Nabiki said. "Anyways, what about him?"

"Well it turns out he and Lia have developed feelings towards each other." Akane.

"How can you tell?" asked Nabiki.

'They slept with each other last night." Akane answered.

There was silence on the other end for a few moments before Nabiki responded. "Ah, you're joking right?"

"Nope." Akane said.

"Is this the same 'I hate boys' Lia that was a carbon copy of her mother?"

"Ha ha." Akane deadpanned.

"So what happened?" Nabiki asked.

"As near as I can tell, Lia discovered that boys have their good points." Akane said. "That, and Naota isn't your usual hormone -overloaded hentai like you normally find around."

"Yeah, hes a trained from birth killer." Nabiki quipped.

"Not by his choice." Akane said. "Hes actually a perfect gentleman."

'Well, thats a plus." Nabiki said. "So, does Ranma know what happened?"

"Yeah." Akane.

"How did he react?"

"How do you think?" Akane asked. "He was about to kill Naota. Fortunately Lia and I intervened."

"Lucky for him."Nabiki said dryly.

"I got Ranma calmed down and we talked it over." Akane said. "We told them to wait before going any further."

"Do you think they listened to you?" asked Nabiki.

"Not in the least." Akane said.

'Well, look at this way: At least hes not like Ranma was." said Nabki.

"No, he's not." Akane said. "Though Christian is getting to be like his father when it comes to causing chaos."

"Oh lucky you." Nabiki said. "You could try your hand at managing Chisu for a day."

"Is he taking after Tatewaki?" Akane asked.

"No, me." Nabiki said. "And I think he's doing it legally too."

"Kami help us." Akane said as Ranma came in. "Well, I have to go. Nap time just finished."

"Okay. Talk to you later Akane." Nabiki said.

"Talk to you later Nabs." Akane said before she hung up.

"Nabiki called?" asked Ranma.

'Yeah." Akane said with a nod. "Theres a new photo in the printer for the album."

"Oh, thanks." Ranma said as he turned. Stepping into the computer room Ranma saw the photo and picked it up. "Okay..." he said as he looked at the photo.

Walking back into the main room he got out the photo album.

"What do you think?" Akane asked.

"I like it." Ranma answered. "I think Naota has discovered the dangers of sunbeams."

"I thought you would." Akane said.

Lias ear twitched as she slowly woke up from her nap. Opening her eyes Lia 'nyaowed' as she looked around for what had woke her up. Looking up she saw the sun had moved up onto the wall.

'Nyaowing' at the sun beam for moving and interfering with her nap Lia laid her head back down and dozed for a few minutes before Naota stirred and woke her back up again.

"Lia?"

Getting a 'nyaow' in response he tried again.

"Lia, wake up."

"Nyaow..." Lia said as she stood up. "I was sleeping."

"I know." Naota answered as he sat up.

"My sunbeam moved." Lia said as she looked up at the wall.

"They do that." Naota said as he massaged some life back into his shoulder.

"Who wants tuna?" Matoko called from the kitchen.

Stepping out into the main room Matoko was surprised to see the two Neko -ken influenced and one cat demon sitting at the table. All three were purring and looking at her expectantly as she place the lunch tray on the table.

Their purring increased as they dug in.

'Sounds like a lions den." Akane said as she came into the main room.

"Thats 'cause I made tuna." Matoko answered.

"Ah." said Akane as she sat down.

By the time Matoko and Akane had halfway finished their meals the others had three helpings. Ranma smiled as he leaned back from the table.

"Ahh...delicious as always Matoko." he said.

"Thank you." Matoko answered.

'I don't know what we're gonna do when you get married and move out on your own." Ranma added.

'I'm sure you'll manage." Matoko answered. "You are a good cook all by yourself after all Ranma." Matoko pointed out.

"True." Ranma concurred.

"What about me?" Lia asked. "What about when I move out?"

"I'm sure we'll manage." Ranma answered. "Why do you ask?"

"Well, Naota and I talked about it, andwe'dreallyliketogetmarried." Lia said in a rush.

Ranmas response was to blink and look at his daughter with a vacant look on his face.

'Daddy?" Lia asked. "Are you mad?"

Ranma thumped his head forwards onto the table. "No, I'm not mad." he said. Sighing he continued. "Lia, why did you have to grow up?"

"I'm sorry Daddy." Lia said as she rubbed her fathers back. "I didn't mean to."

"I know Lia." Ranma said. "I know."

Putting down her chopsticks Akane cleared her throat. "So, when are you two planning to have the big day?" she asked.

"In the spring." answered Lia.

"After we graduate." Naota added.

Akane nodded as Matoko joined the conversation.

"So we have plenty of time to plan the wedding, right?" she asked.

"Yeah.' answered Lia. "But we want a simple one, nothing fancy."

"I know." Akane said as she stood up. "But we still have to plan it, don't we Matoko?"

"Yes." answered Matoko as she smiled and stood up.

Lia blinked as her sister and mother looked at her and smiled.

"Nyaow?" she asked before they walked around the table to her.

Ranma looked up as Matoko and Akane each grabbed an arm and dragged Lia off into the interior of the house. When they were gone he looked at Naota.

"What have I got myself into?" asked Naota.

"The Saotome family." Ranma answered. "Better buckle up, cause it's going to be a wild ride."

"Help."
