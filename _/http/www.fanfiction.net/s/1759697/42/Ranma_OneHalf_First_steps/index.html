Ok...here we go. The plane just took off out of Waterloo International Airport. I'll catch the flight to Tokyo in . This all started about 3 weeks ago when my parents died. The next day was the reading of the will. Me, 3 aunts, 5 uncles, and 2 close friends of the family, and a liar...oops... I mean a lawyer. Well, I have known most all of my life that I am adopted. They handed me a folder that was at least 4 inches thick of information on the 3 of them. There is Naota Sano, aka the little brother, who ain't so little. He is an OK student and not involved in many activities but apparently is the one one I've been playing with on the Quake servers. Kamone Sano, aka the father, a dumb schmuck as I understand. He runs a print shop, a rather successful one as I understand. Dokuohtei Sano, aka the grandfather, Owns/runs the print shop, and is a master at Shogi, whatever that is. I know the area where we will be living. Nerima is an odd little area, I think I can survive it. Goodnight.

It was jam -packed at the Sano apartment as the younger members of the Anything Goes School and the Hagakure School sat down to figure out how to integrate Kenji into the Sano household. Presiding over the meeting was Grandpa and Kamone Sano. After setting out a drink tray and some finger foods thoughtfully prepared by Matoko they set down to business.

"So..." Naota began "Any ideas?"

The group seated around the table were silent as they thought over the problem. After a few moments Kenji was the first to speak.

"Well, if it helps I really don't have that much stuff." he began. "Just my computer rig, my drafting table, my art, and my clothes."

"You gonna be sharing a room?" Christian asked.

Naota shrugged. "Looks like. Not that I mind." he said. "I'll have to clean out a space in my room and go with bunk beds."

Lia and Mamimi shivered at the mention of Naotas room.

"Well, don't expect us to help with that. We ain't going near it." Lia said as Mamimi nodded in agreement.

Naota snorted as he looked at the two girls. "I heard the way you two screamed. I wouldn't dare." he said. "though the hard part is going to be rigging another computer setup in my room. I wish we were moving into our own apartment."

Lia glanced at Naota as he trailed off. "What is it?" she asked.

Naota blinked and glanced at Lia. "We do have an apartment." he said. he then glanced over at his grandfather before continuing. "Hey Grandpa, are we using the apartment above the shop for anything important?"

Grandpa Sano blinked as he looked up from the shogi board. "Ah, no, not really. All we have in there is stuff we haven't gotten around to throwing away yet." he said. "I've been meaning to clean it out."

"How about we clean it out and Kenji and I move in?" Naota asked. "It's just the right size for two teenagers."

"Plus my father won't be anywhere near it." Kenji added.

Grandpa Sano nodded and looked at Kamone. "Well Kamone? What do you think?" he asked.

Kamone scratched his chin for a moment as he thought about it. "Well, he does need to learn how live on his own. And Kenji does have a good point." he said thoughtfully. "And we do need someone at the shop to look over it during the off hours. The alarm company we have is a waste of money anyways."

"Good point." Grandpa Sano said as he moved a piece. "A Hagukure -trained cat demon makes for an excellent alarm system. And he's better armed than the security guards the company uses."

Naota rolled his eyes as he listened to his grandfather and father talk. "You know guys, not everyone has to armed to the molars."

"Well, in Nerima it's a necessity." Grandpa Sano retorted.

"Oh, be quiet-"Naota began before stopping in mid sentence.

"What is it?" Grandpa Sano asked.

"Theres someone at the door." Naota answered.

A knock at the door confirmed what Naota said as he got up.

"You expecting someone?" his father asked as he walked to the door.

'No. And it's not anyone I recognize either." Naota said as he looked out the peephole. "It's some girl. Probably a wrong address."

Opening the door Naota came face to face with the person on the other side. It was a girl, in her late teens or early twenties with her hair braided and put into several ponytails. She was wearing a bright blue tank top and baggy black jeans with lots of chains hanging from them. Behind her was an over sized bag seemingly packed to the max.

"She looks a lot like dad...'Naota thought before he spoke. "Can I help you?"

The girl blinked and looked at Naota fearfully for a second before she responded.

"I-i-i-is this the Sano residence?" she asked in a heavy American accent.

"Okay, now this is starting to get what I would call 'odd'."Naota thought. "Ah, yes it is. Why do you ask?"

"I-I'm looking for a Kamone Sano." the girl said.

"Oh."Naota said as he stepped aside. "Come on in then."

"Than you." the girl said as she stepped inside.

"You're welcome." Naota answered as he closed the door. "What's your name by the way?"

"Bre. Bre Johnson." the girl answered. "Whats yours?"

"Naota." Naota answered.

Bre gave Naota an odd look before heading to the living room. Naota noticed this and filed it away for later as he followed her. As they entered everyone looked up at the newcomer.

"Hey Dad, someone wants to see you." Naota said as he and Bre sat down at the table.

"Oh, hello. And who might you be?" Kamone asked.

"Bre." Bre answered. Reaching into one of the cavernous pockets of her jeans she pulled an envelope out. "And this is for you."

Taking the envelope from Bre Kamone opened it and began to read. As he did that Naota began to read his fathers body language and analyzed what he saw.

"So, you're an American." Lia said as an opener.

Bres eyes went a little wide at the sight of Lias cursed form. "Ah, yes I am." she said as she nodded.

"Cool. Now I have someone else to talk to."Lia answered, dropping her Japanese accent and in English.

Bres eyes went wide at Lias response and she pointed at Lia. "You- you're an American!" she said in English.

Lia nodded. "Yep. And proud of it."

Bre smiled slightly and held out her hand. "So am I." she said. "Bre Johnson."

Lia returned the smile as she shook Bres hand. "Lia Saotome." she said. "What brings you here?"

Bre shrugged and jerked her thumb at Kamone. "Ask him. He'll be able to tell you, right 'Dad'?"

Lias eyes bugged out and Naota glanced over at the two of them.

"Dad?' You telling her about your father?" Naota asked, only getting half of what was said.

Lia shook her head and pointed at Kamone. "Naota, I think you ask your father about your family history." she said.

"Family history..." Naota asked, a puzzled tone in his voice. Scowling he reached over and snatched the papers out of his fathers hand. "Gimme those."

"Hey, I was reading those-" Kamone began as Naota began to skim the top document.

'Certificate of adoption-" Naota mumbled as he read. Pausing he looked at Bre for a second before turning his gaze back to his father. "You care explaining why this says I have a long -lost sister, father?" he asked, holding up the sheaf of papers.

Lia, Bre, Christian and Mamimi scooted away from Naota as his gold -colored chi flowed around him. Swallowing nervously Kamone looked at his son.

"Well, it's a long story..." Kamone began.

"So start talking.' Naota growled.

'You see, your mother and I were in high school, and I got her pregnant." Kamone began. Everyone in the room with the exception of Christian and Mamimi rolled their eyes as Kamone continued. "And the clan elders, well, the elders at that time were a bunch of conservative throwbacks and they didn't approve."

"And so they made you put her up for adoption.' Naota said, pointing at his newly discovered sister.

"Actually they wanted Haruko to have an abortion, but I said that of all the sins he was going to commit, killing his children wasn't going to be one of them." Grandpa Sano interjected.

"Anyways, they agreed to allow us to put her up for adoption.' Kamone said. "I made sure she would end up someplace far away from the Hagakure."

"I was wondering about that..." Bre murmured to herself.

"And the reason why you didn't tell me?" Naota asked.

"Because it slipped my mind?" Kamone suggested.

Grandpa Sano buried his face in his hand as Naotas chi began to build up and flow around him. Items on shelves began to rattle and shake his right eye began to twitch slightly.

"Bre, do you have anything to say to our father at the moment?" Naota asked, his voice dead calm.

Bre, her mind completely off track by what was happening shook her head no. "N-n-no." she stammered.

"Okay." Naota answered.

Everyone in the apartment jumped as Naota blurred and Kamone was slammed into the wall face -first. Taking several deep breaths Naota calmed himself down and looked at his grandfather.

"I'm going to pack a bag and stay at the Saotomes until I have the apartment cleaned out, okay?" Naota said.

Grandpa Sano nodded and Naota tuned on his heel to go into his room. After he left Bre looked at her newly discovered grandfather incredulously.

"You're okay with that?" she asked.

"Yes. He needs his own space right now." Grandpa Sano answered. glancing at his son who was embedded face first into the plaster he continued. "And besides, if he stayed here, odds are he'd kill Kamone for being such a bloody idiot."

"Oh." Bre said in a deadpan voice.

"Do you have a hotel?" Grandpa Sano asked. "You can stay here if you wish. Our couch is quite comfortable."

"Or you can stay at our place." Lia offered. 'We won't mind."

Bre thought for several seconds. Glancing at Kamone embedded in the wall she sighed. "Thank you, but I'll stay with her until I can get a place." she said. pointing to Kamone she continued. "After what he said I want to kill him."

Grandpa Sano smiled and nodded. "I understand.. Everyone usually does eventually."

"Great!" Lia said as she clapped her hands. "You can stay in the guest room."

"Thank you."Bre said.

"Hey, where's Naota gonna sleep?" Christian asked.

'Your room." Lia answered.

"Okay." Christian said with a shrug.

"And if you try anything..." Lia began.

"I'll make you look like Pippy Long stockings." Naota finished as he stepped out with a packed bag. "You coming Kenji?"

Kenji shook his head. "No, I'll stay here thank you. Its a second home to me, and I need to feel safe right now." he said.

"Okay." Naota said with a nod. "Feel free to play as much Quake as you wish. I don't want you going through withdrawal on me."

"Okay. Thanks." Kenji answered.

"You're welcome.' Naota answered. "Now lets go."

Walking alongside Lia Bre kept glancing put the corner of her eye at Lia.

"Something the matter?" Lia asked.

"Huh-no. I'm fine, thanks for asking." Bre answered.

"Oh. You kept looking at me so I was just wondering." Lia said.

"Oh, that." Bre said. "I was wondering why you don't have markings like him." she added, pointing to Naota.

"Oh, that." Lia said with a wave of her hand. "He has those 'cause he's a cat -demon and I just have a mixed curse."

'I got hit with two curses at the same time." Lia explained. "From cursed spring water. One was 'Spring of Drowned Girl', the other was 'Spring of Drowned Cat.' I fell into a barrel of the water from the girl spring, then when I was climbing out some water from the cat spring fell in."

"Oh." Bre said. "Why were you near those barrels?"

Lia sighed and pointed to her little brother walking ahead of them. "I was rescuing him." she said. "But he ended up getting cursed himself. Watch."

Bre watched as Christian walked along, and sure enough as they passed by a yard a stream of water from a garden hose came arcing over the wall and smacked Christian on the head. Instantly black hair turned bright red and clothes became considerably baggier.

Bres jaw went slack as she pointed at Christian.

"He- he- he- he- turned-" she said, her brain not comprehending what had just happened.

"Actually, technically it's she now." Lia said.

"I'm a boy!" Christian snapped back.

"But try to refer to Christian as male. Its a sore for him." Lia added.

Bre nodded absently as they continued walking. They walked in silence for a few moments before Lia nudged Bre in the arm.

"So," she began. "How old are you?"

"Wha-oh. Twenty -three." Bre answered.

"My parents died, and it was in their will that I finish my education with my biological family." Bre answered.

"English." Lia answered. "I want some people around here to at least speak it properly."

Naota shrugged, "Right now, just graduating high school is my immediate goal. After that, well I was gonna take business management so I can help with the print shop."

Walking over to Bre he went to the opposite side of the tarp and picked it up. With Bre at the other end they lifted the tarp off and their jaws dropped at what was under it.

That turned out to be a '96 Suzuki GSX -R600 and a Vespa scooter.

"I don't believe it." Naota said, his voice full of disbelief.

'What?" Bre asked.

"He kept them." answered Naota. "Dads bike and Moms scooter."

"Our mother rode that?" Bre asked, pointing to the Vespa.

"Yeah." Naota answered with a nod of his head. "Dad said that when he rode it, it was a religious experience."

"Religious experience?" Bre asked.

"Yeah." Naota said with a nod. "He said something about a little red button."

"She put nitrous(2) on it?" Bre asked.

"I guess." Naota said with a shrug. "But we can check fr that later. Right now we have to get them downstairs."

"Right. I'll get a couple of the guys-" Bre began.

"I got it." Naota said as he stepped up to the Vespa. "I have a demons strength remember."

Bre gulped as Naota casually picked up the Vespa and walked to the door, carrying the scooter like it was nothing. Carefully he threaded the scooter out the door and onto the landing. Lifting it up he began to carry it down the stairs.

Stepping out of the bedroom with a box of junk Lia saw Bre standing staring at the door open mouthed.

"You okay?" Lia asked.

"Yeah." Bre said with a nod. "I just got reminded that my brother is a demon."

"Oh. What did he do?" Lia asked.

"He picked up a scooter like it was a cardboard box and hauled it down the stairs." Bre answered.

"Oh, yeah. He can do that." Lia said before heading to the door.

Bre gaped as Lia walked out the door.

"Well, that was the last of it." Naota said as he stepped back into the apartment. "That was the last box."

"I still say you should have left the bikes up here." Hideo said. "They made great decoration."

"Hideo, unlike you, some people like to have their machinery outside." Naota sighed. "Besides, we have to replace the carpet and they would have been in the way."

"We doing that tomorrow?" Eichiro asked.

"I was thinking we could rip it up tomorrow, them we could paint." Naota answered. "You can get the paint we need, right?" he asked Fujimaro.

'Yeah." Fujimaro answered with a nod. "Kenji gave me a color list, and my dad has all of the colors in stock."

"Good. Everybody meet up here after school tomorrow?" Naota asked. Getting a round of affirmatives he nodded. "Okay. See you then."

"Okay, now lets go home." Bre said. "I don't know about you guys but I want to go get cleaned up."

"Ditto." Lia added. "I've gotta wash all this crud out of my hair."

"In that case you had better use the furo first." Matoko told Bre. "It takes forever to wash Lias hair."

"Uh, yeah." Bre said as she looked at Lias pigtail, which hung to past her knees.

Thus they began to walk home to the Saotome household, Christian and Mamimi ranging forwards of the group but staying in sight. Walking alongside Kenji Bre cast a glance up at him.

"Can I ask you a question?" Bre asked.

"Yes." Kenji answered.

"What were you and Mamimi doing wandering around the apartment?"

Kenji shrugged lopsidedly as he walked. "I was laying out the decor of the apartment and she was taking notes. It was the only thing I can do right now." he said. "That and playing Quake."

"So my brother mentioned." Bre said. "By the way, how good are you?"

"I'm good." answered Kenji.

His answer piqued Bres interest. "How good?" she asked. "He who Ownz good?"

"I like to think so." Kenji answered. "Though you're a close second."

"Thanks Kenji- waitaminute." Bre said as Kenjis answer registered on her brain. "The only way you'd know that is if you're-"

Kenji nodded as they walked along. Bre worked her mouth up and down a few times as she tried to figure out what to say.

"You?" she finally asked.

"Uh huh." Kenji answered.

"Huh." Bre said. "I would have never figured it'd be you."

"I get that a lot." Kenji answered.

"And Naota also tells me you're an artist." Bre added.

"Yeah." Kenji said. "People say I'm pretty good."

Bre arced an eyebrow as she looked at Kenji. "Like you're good at Quake?"

"Well..." Kenji began "Actually, not to brag, but I'm better."

"Oy." Bre said. "I want to see your work."

"How about tomorrow?" Kenji asked as they reached a cross street.

"Sounds good." Bre answered as Kenji and Naota turned left.

"You guys go on ahead. I gotta pick up some things at my place." Naota said as they walked away.

"Don't be too late. Matokos cooking tonight." Lia said as they started walking.

"Don't worry. I wont." Naota answered before they were out of sight.

Naotas skin prickled into goosebumps as an evening breeze blew down the street.

"Autumn's coming." Naota commented.

"Yeah." Kenji answered.

'Hows the arm?" Naota asked, glancing at Kenjis cast.

"Hurts a bit. Itches a lot too." Kenji answered. "Though I hope Lias uncle was right and that it can come off soon. If I don't draw soon I'm gonna go crazy."

"Quakes not helping?" Naota asked.

"A little." Kenji admitted. "But not enough. I gotta draw. At least Matoko can still write."

Naota chuckled at his friends predicament. "At least your thinking okay." he said.

"How about you?" Kenji asked. "How is you having a sister sitting with you?"

Naota sighed and thought for several long moments.

"It's...it's...it's still sinking in." he answered. "Finding out I have a sister is cool and all, and Bres a great person, but..."

"But?"

"But I got a reminder just how much I don't have." Naota answered. "I thought about my- scratch that, our mother for the first time in a while."

"At least you have a father worthy of the name." Kenji said, hefting his cast. "I wonder if my brother and I are the only ones in our family worth a damn."

"Hey, he did try to keep your parents focused on him." Naota pointed out. "I'd say that answers your question."

"True." Kenji agreed. "And he helped us with the Empty Year." he added, looking at his left wrist.

"Yeah." Naota agreed. "Speaking of your brother, have you talked to him?"

"Yeah. I emailed him yesterday." Kenji said with a nod. "And I talked to him on the phone."

"And?"

"And as soon as he can chisel out a date he'll come over." Kenji answered. "His boss is running him ragged."

"Poor guy." Naota said. "How did he react?"

"He's pissed and he wants to kill our father." Kenji answered. "I walked him out of seeing the folks for a while."

"Good idea." Naota said.

"I told him you have dibs on father." Kenji said as they rounded the corner.

"Thanks." Naota said.

"Hey, you did scare him, so I figure its the least I could do." Kenji said as they approached their apartment building.

"You watch my back and I watch your back." Naota answered with a shrug.

Kenji nodded in agreement as they entered the apartment complex.
