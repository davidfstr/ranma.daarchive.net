Fics that Rakhal has liked while compiling the Index
This section of the index is where I've listed the fics that I've enjoyed reading while compiling the Index. At present, these are only the fics that I've liked while doing the last 98 updates (fics read in the last 201 weeks). In time I will work through the index listing other fics that I've liked.

It should be noted that the only reason a fic is listed here is that I quite enjoyed reading it. It does not mean that you will enjoy reading it, part of what affects whether I enjoy reading a story is my particular character and theme likes. For instance, my favourite characters are Ranma and Akane. And I have a particular dislike for fics where Ranma is split into a male and female. That doesn't mean that no fic that has this theme will ever be listed here, but it does mean that it will have to relatively be a better fic for me to still enjoy it.

Not being listed here does NOT mean a fic is bad, it just means that I didn't really enjoy it when I read it. You may very well think that it is wonderful.
--Rakhal
