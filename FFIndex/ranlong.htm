Long fics. The stories that you can get stuck into
This section of the index lists major stories. Those fics you can really get your teeth into and that will keep you amused for more than a few minutes. I have made no value judgement as to what fics should appear in this section, the only criterion here is length.

Stories listed here are split into 3 categories. Stories 1000 pages long or longer, stories from 500 to 999 pages long and stories from 250 to 499 pages. The length of a story is defined as the total length of the story and all its sequels/sidestories. A story with 4 entries in the index will only be listed once here, the entry being to that series' first part.
