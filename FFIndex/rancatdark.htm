This page lists dark stories. I define a dark story as a story where either someone dies in a non-comedic manner, or someones life is drastically changed for the worse. For instance, a rape would usually be regarded as dark.

Only stories listed in the last 85 updates are listed here. In time I will work through the index listing other dark stories.
